Frequently Asked Questions

Is food included in the booking?

Food is not included, but all villas have fully equipped kitchens. We can also arrange for an in-villa chef or catering service on request.

How far is the villa from Mumbai/Pune?

Our villas are approximately 85 km from Mumbai (2 hours) and 65 km from Pune (1.5 hours) via the Mumbai-Pune Expressway.

Can we host parties or events?

Yes, small celebrations and gatherings are welcome. Please inform us in advance so we can make necessary arrangements.

Is the pool heated?

Select villas feature heated pools. Please check the villa details or contact us for specific information.
